Coaching

Private coaching sessions designed for white adoptive parents navigating the specific, real-time challenges of raising a Black child. No scripts. No judgment. Just honest conversation with someone who has lived the inside of this experience.

Coaching is for you if:

  • You want to talk through a specific situation or conversation you're facing
  • Your child is showing signs of identity struggle and you don't know how to respond
  • You're preparing for a difficult conversation and want to get it right
  • You want honest feedback, not reassurance, from someone who lived this
  • You know something is missing in your family's approach, but can't name it
